FoxSearch

We present a prototype system for providing quick information on common and popular queries through text messaging as well as onsite. The system receives a free text input in Roman script. The system is designed mainly for low-cost mobile phones having text-only messaging facility but without Internet and native language support. The target users are primarily semi-literate people who may not have sufficient knowledge in English. The output is thus translated in the language of user query (in this case Hindi) as transliterated text.
